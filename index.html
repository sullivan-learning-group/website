Helping data professionals adapt to a rapidly changing technology landscape.

The technology landscape for data professionals is shifting fast. AI, cloud platforms, and new analytical tools are redefining what it means to work with data. Deep expertise in a single tool or role is not enough. The practitioners who thrive will be the ones who build broad adaptable skill sets across AI, data science, data analytics, and cloud technologies.

I've spent over 30 years working across data engineering, AI, machine learning, data strategy and cloud architecture. I've written certification study guides for Google Cloud, built courses for LinkedIn Learning, Udemy, and Pearson/Coursera, and helped organizations and practitioners navigate this evolving landscape. The through line: helping data professionals build the skills that matter now and the adaptability to keep up with what's next.

Below you'll find my books and courses, organized by topic. Whether you're preparing for a Google Cloud certification, sharpening your SQL skills, or developing expertise in AI and data strategy, browse what's relevant and start building.
